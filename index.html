Keeping Your Winter Glow with Scarlett Johansson

By Admin ┃ December 18, 2022 ┃ 6 Min Read

Hey everyone! Today, I wanted to share a few tips on using lipstick and contour, inspired by my favorite celebrity makeup looks. Scarlett Johansson is known for her gorgeous, radiant skin and bold lip color, and I always love seeing her red carpet beauty looks for inspiration.

First, let's talk about lipstick. Scarlett often opts for bold, statement-making shades, like classic red or deep berry. To get the most out of your lipstick, it's important to prep your lips first by exfoliating and moisturizing. This will help the color go on smoothly and last longer. When applying your lipstick, use a lip brush for precise application and to help the color stay in the lines. You can also blot your lips with a tissue and dust a little powder over them to set the color and make it more long-lasting.

Next, let's talk about contour. Scarlett has a beautiful, defined jawline and cheekbones, and contouring is a great way to enhance your natural features and add dimension to your face. When contouring, it's important to use the right shade for your skin tone – you want a color that's slightly darker than your natural skin tone, but not too dark or orange. Use a contour brush or beauty sponge to apply the product in a "3" shape on either side of your face – starting at your temples, down your cheekbones, and back up to your jawline. Blend the contour out well to avoid any harsh lines.
